About Horsemanship Art Poetry

Some horses are trained.
Some horses train the human.

— Praveen Kumar

This website is a quiet record of years spent beside horses — observing their intelligence, their emotions, and the strange kind of honesty that exists between a horse and the human who truly listens.

It is also a place for writing, philosophy, art, and reflections that grew out of that world.
