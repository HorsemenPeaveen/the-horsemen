About The Horseman

Praveen Kumar is a natural horseman with more than 10½ years of experience working with horses including warmblood stallions. His work focuses on calm communication, emotional awareness and building trust between horse and rider without force. He has also trained students and continues to explore horsemanship through writing, philosophy and art.
