Art

Art has always been a way to observe the horse beyond movement and training. Through painting and drawing, I try to understand the structure, emotion and spirit of the horse.

Mediums

• Digital Art • Watercolour Paintings • Oil Paintings • Graphite Drawings

Most of the works focus on the anatomy, movement and emotional presence of horses.
